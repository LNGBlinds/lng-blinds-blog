Honeycomb Shades in Valley Stream: What to Know

Honeycomb shades, also known as cellular shades, are one of the most popular window treatments for Long Island homes.

Why homeowners choose honeycomb shades

  • Excellent insulation for hot summers and cold winters
  • Clean, modern look
  • Great for bedrooms, living rooms, and offices
